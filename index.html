Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this feastive focaccia appetizer topped with a sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  1. 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  2. 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  3. 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  4. 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  5. 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow oniones, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamie vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixerwith a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully assorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, thought I sometimes knead ut a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last on the flour...
